Dominican Republic

The Dominican Republic occupies the eastern two-thirds of Hispaniola in the Caribbean. Known for stunning beaches like Punta Cana and rich merengue music, it blends Spanish colonial history with vibrant Afro-Caribbean culture. Santo Domingo, its capital, holds the Americas' oldest European settlement. Sugar, tourism, and baseball define its modern identity.
